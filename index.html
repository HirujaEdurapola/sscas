About Us

At the Astronomical Society of Sri Sumangala College - Panadura, we unite curious minds with a shared passion for the cosmos. Our society is dedicated to exploring the mysteries of the universe, from the twinkling stars above to the latest astronomical discoveries. We offer engaging activities, hands-on observation sessions, and discussions that fuel both academic inquiry and lifelong wonder. Join us as we venture into the night sky, learn from one another, and inspire a deeper understanding of the universe.

Seeking Wisdom in the Cosmos, Finding Joy in Knowledge.

New Year Morning Stargazing 

April 14, 2025 | Astrophotography

On the morning of the Sinhala and Tamil New Year, a member of our society observed the sky during the auspicious time. The goal was to spot the zodiac constellation Aries, which is important during this season. It was a peaceful and meaningful start to the New Year.
